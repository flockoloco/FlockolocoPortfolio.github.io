Go To Bed


Go To Bed is a 3D single-player, first person, stealth horror game for Windows PC and MacOS.
This game is all about stealth and horror, thus the player controls a child stuck in their nightmares that needs to search through a mansion to find their teddy bear.
Throughout this mission, the player needs to hide from 3 different monsters that prowl the house searching for the kid.
The game is divided into multiple small objectives in order to guide the player throughout the experience.
