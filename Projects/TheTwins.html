The Twins


The Twins is a 2D dungeon crawler game for PC in conjunction with a companion app based around an idle farming game for android.
This game makes use of randomly generated rooms from the elements of a dungeon crawler and enemies that fire an incredible amount of projectiles from the elements of a bullet hell.
All of that added with a side-kick companion app that smoothly makes the game slightly easier.
